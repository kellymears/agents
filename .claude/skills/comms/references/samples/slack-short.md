# Samples: Short Slack Messages

These are real examples of quick, casual Slack messages. They show how the voice works at its most compressed: lowercase, fragments, links without ceremony, genuine enthusiasm without performance.

> The people who make this make glow which is one of my absolute favs

Notice:
- Lowercase `i` in casual context, proper case when the thought is substantive
- Two-dot ellipses (`..`) not three
- Links dropped inline without "here's a link" or "check this out"
- "guise" — playful, not forced
- "absolute favs" — genuine enthusiasm, informal vocabulary
- Starts with the point, always
