Notice:
- Opens with @mention and what he's already looked at
- States his current understanding/workflow before asking the question
- The question is specific and answerable (yes/no with context)
- No greeting, no "hope you're doing well", no sign-off
- Technical terms used naturally (make, stash-and-update, miso)
- The "(edited)" tag is a Slack artifact — real messages get edited
